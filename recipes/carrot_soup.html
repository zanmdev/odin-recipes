Instapot Carrot Soup

Description:

Instant Pot Carrot Soup – a creamy pressure cooker soup that is so easy to make! This cozy spicy and sweet carrot soup is the perfect comfort food for cold days and ready in under 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 medium onion chopped
  • 1 clove garlic minced
  • 1 tbsp minced ginger
  • 1lb carrot, peeled and chopped
  • 1/4 teaspoon brown sugar
  • kosher salt
  • ground black pepper
  • 2 cups low-sodium broth (chicken or veggie)
  • 13.5 ounce can unsweetened coconut milk
  • 1 tbsp siracha
  • cilantro leaves

Steps

  1. Press the saute button, when hot add butter and oil. Then add the onions and saute for about 2-3 minutes. Add the garlic and ginger, saute for 1 more minute.
  2. Add chopped carrots and brown sugar. Season with salt and pepper. Saute for 2 more minutes.
  3. Stir in chicken broth, coconut milk, and 1 tbsp Sriracha sauce (or more if you love really spicy food!).
  4. Chop the veggies until roughly the same size as the black beans.
  5. Cook at high pressure for 6 minutes. Natural pressure release for 5-10 minutes, then quick release.
  6. Use an immersion blender to purée the soup or let it cool slightly, then purée in a blender until smooth.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve garnished with coriander.
