Instapot Spaghetti

Description:

This One-Pot Spaghetti with Turkey Meat Sauce is made with whole wheat pasta in the pressure cooker â€“ hands down, the quickest and easiest way to get dinner on the table while making the whole family happy!

Ingredients

  • 1lb Ground Turkey
  • 3/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/4 cup diced onion
  • 1 clove minced garlic
  • 25oz jar tomato sauce
  • 2 cups water
  • 8 ounces whole wheat spaghetti
  • grated parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Saute ground turkey with salt
  2. Add onions and garlic. Cook until softened 3-4 mins
  3. Add the tomato sauce, water and spaghetti
  4. Cover and cook on high for 9 minutes
  5. Quick release to stop the pasta from cooking and top with grated cheese
